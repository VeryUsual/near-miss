# Near Miss

## Play
Play the game [here](https://todo.todo/todo) on itch.io.

## Controls
WASD to Move

Left Shift to Sprint

F to Open Card Deck

## Card Deck
Press F to open your Card Deck. In there, you can activate abilities that help you survive longer.
